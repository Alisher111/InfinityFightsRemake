INFINITY FIGHTS Rules

The User Has the Right To:

  • Suggest battles.
  • Comment on profiles respectfully.
  • Use strong language moderately without insulting others.
  • Attach images, videos, and audio recordings that do not contain hate speech, explicit content, or harassment.

The Subscriber has no Right To:

  • Insult other users, characters, franchises, religions, nations, or the project itself.
  • Post offensive or explicit media.
  • Spam discussion threads.
  • Flood comments with off-topic content.

The Editor Has the Right To:

  • Publish approved battles.
  • Create future battle drafts.
  • Maintain hashtags and categories.
  • Flood comments with off-topic content.
  • Assist with content creation when approved by the administrator.

The Editor Has No Right To:

  • Publish battles that do not meet project standards.
  • Delete or modify battles without discussion.
  • Edit another editor's work without permission.
  • Delete scheduled content without notifying the author.
  • Modify project themes without administrator approval.
  • Flood comments with off-topic content.

Penalties for Subscribers, who breaks the rules:

  • First violation — warning.
  • Repeated violation — 2-day ban.
  • Third violation — 1-week ban.
  • Fourth violation — 1-month ban.
  • Further violations may result in a permanent ban.
