FAQ

What is "battle"?

A crossover fight between characters from different universes.

Poll Battle:

  • Users vote for winners
  • Arguments are allowed
  • Debates must follow rules

Death Battle:

  • Official creators decide result
  • We only publish content

DBX:

  • Winner chosen by vote

How can you compare different universes?

Based on lore, feats, official information and context of battle.

Game mechanics vs reality?

Game mechanics are ignored unless specified.

Powerful gear in story?

Only stated equipment is used in battle.

Does universe advantage matter?

No. Battles occur in neutral crossover conditions.
