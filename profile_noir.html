Black Noir — the silent assassin of The Seven.

Black Noir

Biography

Black Noir is a mysterious member of The Seven, used as Vought’s silent executioner for covert operations.

Strength

  • Meta-human physical strength
  • Can overpower and lift armed opponents
  • High destructive melee force

Speed & Reflexes

  • High-speed movement comparable to vehicles
  • Extremely fast assassination execution
  • Can eliminate targets before reaction time

Endurance

  • Bullet-resistant durability (likely armored physiology)
  • Survived explosions and energy impacts
  • Continues fighting despite severe damage

Weakness

Severe nut allergy causing anaphylactic shock, which can incapacitate or potentially kill him without treatment.

Skills

  • Master stealth assassin
  • Expert infiltration and surveillance
  • Close-quarters killing specialist

Experience

Veteran operative responsible for eliminating terrorists, superhumans, and covert targets for Vought.

Equipment

  • Kevlar combat suit
  • Combat knives
  • Throwing knives
  • Short blade weapons
  • Medical stimulant/antihistamine
