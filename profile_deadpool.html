Deadpool

Biography

Deadpool (born Wade Winston Wilson) is a Canadian freelance mercenary who became a superhuman with an accelerated healing factor from the Mutant Workshop program.

Strength

  • Superhuman-level physical strength
  • Can tear armored materials with force
  • High melee combat effectiveness

Speed & Reflexes

  • Enhanced reaction speed
  • Bullet-timing combat ability
  • Extreme agility and acrobatics

Endurance

  • Regenerative healing factor
  • Can survive extreme trauma
  • Resists conventional fatal damage

Skills

  • Master marksman and swordsman
  • Expert in improvisational combat
  • Advanced tactical awareness

Equipment

  • Dual katanas
  • Firearms and explosives
  • Utility gear and gadgets
